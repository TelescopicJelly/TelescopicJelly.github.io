Botany

Botany is the study plants in their growth, reproduction, evolution, and diseases. It explores how plants interact with their environment and other living organisms. Modern botany uses advanced tools like genetics and genomics to study how plants grow, reproduce, and adapt, helping improve farming, medicine, environmental care, and resource use.
